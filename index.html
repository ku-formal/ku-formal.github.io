About Me

As of September 2025, I am working as an assistant professor at School of Cybersecurity of Korea University (KU SCS) where I am leading the Formal Software Verification Laboratory. Previously, I served as an assistant professor at GIST from September 2023 to August 2025 and as a research professor at Korea University from September 2022 to August 2023.


Research Interests

I am interested in various research topics for improving security and safety of software. In particular, I mainly focus on developing and using techniques based on SMT-based formal methods.
  • Safety verification for proving the absence of vulnerabilities and errors in software.
  • Symbolic execution for finding vulnerabilities and errors in software.
  • Efficient decision procedures for first-order theories for making formal verification tools more practical.
